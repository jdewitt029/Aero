Familial Home

Video Editing/Augmented Reality
0:21

In this work, I decided to tell a narrative on the relationship between home and identity in my perspective. I created an augmented reality environment using Adobe Aero and created a video walking through that environment. To me, home is where your family is - blood or not. Family also plays into identity as they shape who you become and family is where you are your truest natural self. Family is one of the things I hold dear so I decided tomake this video to show how important it is.
